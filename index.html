FAQ

Can I get my money back if I change my mind?

Yes. Your $5 deposit is fully refundable anytime before you place an order, no questions asked, just email us at hello@buddydesign.co and we'll process your refund.

How does Buddy protect my privacy?

Buddy has no camera. It uses mmWave radar to sense your breathing, heart rate, and movement, and keeps your data on-device.

How can a contactless bedside lamp be more accurate than a smart watch or a ring?

Contactless sensing isn't inherently better than a wearable, it's a different way of measuring the same signals, similar to how hospitals monitor vitals without touching a patient. We validate Buddy's readings against real wearable data every night to keep the accuracy honest.

Does it work with two people in bed?

Right now, Buddy is built and tuned to track one person. We're actively testing how it performs with two people sharing a bed, and we'll update this the moment that's fully validated.

Does it improve my sleep, or just track it?

Both. Buddy tracks what's actually happening each night, then uses that to build your wind-down routine, time your alarm, and get to know you better. Tracking isn't the end goal, it's what makes the rest possible.

How does Buddy differ from other smart lamps?

Most smart lamps only control light. Buddy also senses your sleep without a camera or a wearable, then uses what it learns to build your routine and wake you at the right moment. The light is one part of a system built around your actual sleep.

Do I need an app to use it?

No, everything works by voice and touch, night to night. There's a companion app if you want to dig into your sleep data, but it's optional, not required.

Will blankets or pets affect Buddy's sleep tracking?

Blankets don't block Buddy's radar, so normal bedding doesn't affect tracking. A pet moving nearby can occasionally register as movement, but Buddy is built to focus on your vitals, not just motion, so it's not something that typically throws off your results.
